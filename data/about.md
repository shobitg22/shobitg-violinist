# About Me

Welcome! I'm Shobit G, a professional violinist with a passion for bringing classical and contemporary music to life. With years of experience performing at weddings, corporate events, concerts, and private gatherings, I specialize in creating unforgettable musical experiences.

## My Journey

My journey with the violin began at a young age, and over the years, I've developed a unique style that blends classical techniques with modern interpretations. Whether it's a romantic wedding ceremony, an elegant corporate event, or an intimate private concert, I tailor each performance to match the atmosphere and emotion of your special occasion.

## Performance Style

I offer a diverse repertoire that spans classical masterpieces, Bollywood favorites, contemporary hits, and original compositions. My goal is to connect with the audience through music, creating moments that resonate long after the last note fades.

- Wedding Ceremonies & Receptions
- Corporate Events & Galas
- Private Concerts & Parties
- Studio Recording Sessions
- Music Collaborations
